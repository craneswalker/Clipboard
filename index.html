Purpose of Clipboard

Clipboard is an organization and practice planning tool that allows coaches to keep up to date with the needs of their team and/or players.

Functionality: COACHES

  • Allows Scheduling of Practices, Workouts, etc.
  • Access to drill videos
  • Ranking of players skills
  • Practice Plans from templates, player strength/weaknesses or from scratch
  • Video Capture capability
  • Direct chat w/ players.

Functionality: PLAYERS

  • Request Scheduling of Practices, Workouts, etc.
  • Access to drill videos
  • View ranking of players skills
  • View practice plans
  • Direct chat w/ coach.

Functionality: TEAMS

  • Allows Scheduling of Practices, Workouts, etc.
  • Access to drill videos
  • Stats
  • Ranking of players skills and easy depth charting
  • Ranking of team skills
  • Practice Plans from templates, player or team strength/weaknesses or from scratch
  • Video Capture capability
